I heard there had been a free World ID hat that only a human-backed AI agent could claim, went to check it out, and found that I was already too late.

The official documentation page is now titled `[Sold Out] Claim Your Free Hat`, and the page itself says, “All hats have been claimed.” The free-hat campaign is over.

:::conclusion
As of May 2026, World ID's free-hat campaign has already sold out. By the time I found it, the window had closed.
:::

## What Was Being Given Away?

World's AgentKit includes a way to prove that an AI agent is backed by a real human through World ID.

The free hat was a demo of that idea. In the `Human Required` Shopify store, only human-backed agents registered through AgentBook could receive a 100% discount and claim the hat for free.

In other words, this was not a normal giveaway for people clicking a checkout button themselves. It was a novelty item that could be claimed **only through an AI agent verified by World ID**.

## Can You Still Get One?

At least for this drop, no.

The official docs now state:

> All hats have been claimed. Thanks for the incredible response — stay tuned for future drops.

That leaves the door open for future campaigns, but this particular free hat is gone.

## Why It Was Interesting Anyway

The clever part was not the hat itself. It was the rule behind it: **give a benefit only to an AI agent that can prove it belongs to a real human**.

AgentKit still documents a `free` mode that waives payment for human-backed agents and a `free-trial` mode that makes only the first few uses free. So the hat was also a small public demo of what an agent economy could look like once identity, permissions, and payments start fitting together.

:::note
The hats are gone, but the underlying design remains: allow only human-backed agents through, then grant those agents free or discounted access.
:::

## Summary

I missed the World ID free-hat drop.

Still, the more memorable part is that the experiment has already moved beyond slides and into an actual checkout flow: **rights can now be handed not just to people, but to AI agents acting on behalf of verified people**.

If there is another drop, next time I may need to have the agent waiting before I am.
