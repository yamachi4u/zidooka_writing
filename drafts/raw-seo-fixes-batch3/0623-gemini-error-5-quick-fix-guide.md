<h2 class="wp-block-heading">■ What this error actually is</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>“An error has occurred. (5)” is a <strong>generic internal failure</strong> in Gemini.<br>It mainly appears on the <strong>web version (Chrome/Windows)</strong> and often affects only one model (2.5 Pro or 2.5 Flash).</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Many users report:</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>For the Japanese version of the same issue, see <a href="https://www.zidooka.com/archives/621">this article</a>. If the problem is closer to API quota or model-side exhaustion, <a href="https://www.zidooka.com/archives/3510">Resource has been exhausted</a> may be a better match.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul class="wp-block-list"><!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>PC browser → <strong>Error (5)</strong></li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Smartphone app → works fine</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item --></ul>
<!-- /wp:list -->

<!-- wp:separator -->
<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>
<!-- /wp:separator -->

<!-- wp:heading -->
<h2 class="wp-block-heading">■ Main causes</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:list {"ordered":true} -->
<ol class="wp-block-list"><!-- wp:list-item -->
<li><strong>Corrupted browser session/cache</strong></li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li><strong>A specific model (Pro/Flash) being temporarily down</strong></li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li><strong>Extensions, VPN, or organization-level restrictions</strong></li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li><strong>Google-side outage</strong> (quite common)</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item --></ol>
<!-- /wp:list -->

<!-- wp:separator -->
<hr class="wp-block-separator has-alpha-channel-opacity"/>
<!-- /wp:separator -->

<!-- wp:heading -->
<h2 class="wp-block-heading">■ Fastest fixes (ranked by effectiveness)</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:list {"ordered":true} -->
<ol class="wp-block-list"><!-- wp:list-item -->
<li><strong>Switch the model</strong><!-- wp:list -->
<ul class="wp-block-list"><!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Pro ↔ Flash<br>This alone fixes it in many cases.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item --></ul>
<!-- /wp:list --></li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li><strong>Open Gemini in Incognito mode</strong></li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Clear cache/cookies (Google/Gemini only)</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Restart browser → log in again</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Use the <strong>mobile app</strong> temporarily</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item --></ol>
<!-- /wp:list -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>If none works → it's almost certainly a <strong>server-side issue</strong>, and users report it resolves after hours/days.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->
