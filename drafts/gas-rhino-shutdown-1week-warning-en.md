The old execution engine for Google Apps Script (GAS), "Rhino Runtime",
will completely end support on **January 31, 2026**.

If your GAS project is still running on the Rhino runtime,
**Your scripts will STOP working next week (after February 1st).**

*   Automated emails will stop
*   Spreadsheet integrations will fail
*   Business systems will throw errors

To prevent this, please check your settings **RIGHT NOW**.

## What should I do?

Checking and migrating is very easy. It takes just 3 clicks.

:::step
1.  Open the GAS Editor
2.  Open "Project Settings"
3.  Turn ON "Enable Chrome V8 runtime"
:::

That's it.
